Level 1 Drought Response

Statewide Drought Response

Georgia's Environmental Protection Division has declared a Level 1 Drought Response across the state. CCWA is asking all customers to take immediate steps to conserve water.

Outdoor landscape watering is restricted to the hours before 10 AM and after 4 PM each day.

  • Check for and repair household leaks promptly
  • Limit outdoor watering to early morning or evening
  • Use a broom instead of hose to clean driveways
  • Run full loads in dishwashers and washing machines
  • Turn off the tap while brushing teeth
  • Report visible water waste to CCWA at 770-960-5200

Our Services

Water Distribution

Water Treatment
