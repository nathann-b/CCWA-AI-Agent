Our Services

Home› Services

Clayton County Water Authority (CCWA) provides a comprehensive range of water and wastewater services to residential, commercial, and industrial customers throughout Clayton County, Georgia. We serve over 300,000 people across approximately 155 square miles.

Customer Services

💳

Billing & Payments

Pay online, by phone, by mail, or in person. Set up auto-pay or paperless billing for convenience.

🔌

Start / Stop / Transfer Service

New residents and businesses can establish service; existing customers can transfer or disconnect service.

👤

Online Account Portal

View billing history, track water usage, update contact information, and manage your account 24/7.

🔧

Report an Issue

Report leaks, low pressure, water quality concerns, and other service issues online or by phone.

💰

Financial Assistance

CCWA offers payment arrangements and connects qualifying customers to assistance programs.

📄

Leak Adjustments

If you discover and repair a hidden leak, you may be eligible for a one-time billing adjustment.

Water & Distribution Services

🏭

Water Treatment

CCWA operates advanced treatment facilities that process over 50 million gallons per day, meeting all EPA and Georgia EPD standards.

🚰

Water Distribution

Over 1,200 miles of water mains deliver safe drinking water to every corner of Clayton County 24 hours a day.

🔬

Water Quality Testing

Thousands of quality tests are conducted each year. Results are published in our annual Consumer Confidence Report.

🔢

Meter Services

We install, maintain, and test water meters. Advanced metering infrastructure (AMI) helps customers track usage in real time.

🔗

New Water Taps & Connections

Developers and contractors can apply for new water tap permits and system extensions through our Development Services division.

🚒

Fire Hydrant Services

CCWA maintains all public fire hydrants within the service area and coordinates with local fire departments.

Wastewater & Environmental Services

♻️

Wastewater Collection

CCWA operates and maintains over 900 miles of sewer mains and numerous pump stations serving the county.

🌊

Wastewater Treatment

Our reclamation facilities treat wastewater to levels that exceed state requirements before returning it to local waterways.

🌱

Biosolids Management

Treated biosolids are responsibly managed in accordance with EPA guidelines, supporting beneficial reuse programs.

🔗

New Sewer Taps & Connections

Apply for new sewer connections, permits, and capacity certifications through our Development Services division.

Conservation & Community Programs

🌿

Water Conservation Rebates

Customers may qualify for rebates on WaterSense-certified toilets, irrigation controllers, and washing machines.

🏫

School Education Programs

CCWA educators visit schools across Clayton County to teach students about the water cycle, conservation, and careers in water.

🔍

Free Water Audits

Residential customers can request a free indoor or outdoor water use audit to identify savings opportunities.

📅

Community Workshops

Free workshops on topics such as irrigation efficiency, drought preparedness, and leak detection are held throughout the year.

Have a Question About Our Services?

Our Customer Service team is here to help Monday through Friday, 8:00 AM – 5:00 PM.
